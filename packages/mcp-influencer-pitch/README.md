# @mcpfactory/influencer-pitch

**DFY Influencer Outreach** - Find and pitch relevant influencers automatically

> You provide a URL + niche. We find influencers, research them, craft personalized pitches, and handle outreach.

## What This MCP Does

1. **Analyzes your brand** - Understands your product/service from URL
2. **Finds influencers** - Instagram, TikTok, YouTube in your niche
3. **Filters by fit** - Engagement rate, audience demographics, brand alignment
4. **Researches each** - Recent content, style, collaboration history
5. **Generates personalized pitches** - References their specific content
6. **Sends outreach** - Email or DM with proper follow-ups
7. **Tracks responses** - Reports opens, replies, partnerships
