# @mcpfactory/thought-leader

**DFY Thought Leadership PR** - Get featured in publications as an industry expert

> You provide a URL. We identify thought leadership angles, find relevant journalists, and pitch you as an expert source.

## What This MCP Does

1. **Analyzes your expertise** - From URL, LinkedIn, past content
2. **Identifies angles** - Trending topics where you can contribute
3. **Finds journalists** - Who cover your industry and need sources
4. **Crafts expert pitches** - Position you as a go-to source
5. **Sends outreach** - Personalized to each journalist's beat
6. **Tracks coverage** - Reports when you get quoted/featured
